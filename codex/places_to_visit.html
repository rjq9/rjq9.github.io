Bonehenge

Nearby the city is a gigantic pyramid of bones with intricate carvings. Surrounding it is several gates also made of bones. 

This site, the aptly named "Bonehenge," is of unknown origin, but many archaeologists believe it is from the ancient city of Ghahnna,
which was a prosperous city of cannibalistic spiders until the man-eating spiders conquered the world.
